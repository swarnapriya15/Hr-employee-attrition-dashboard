# HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **HR Employee Attrition Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Power BI**. The dashboard helps analyze employee attrition by identifying key factors that influence employees leaving the organization. Users can interact with the dashboard using filters to gain deeper insights into workforce trends.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To build an interactive dashboard that answers the question:

**"Why are employees leaving the organization?"**

The dashboard enables users to filter data by:
- Department
- Age Group
- Business Travel

---

## 📂 Dataset
**Dataset:** IBM HR Employee Attrition Dataset

The dataset contains employee information such as:
- Age
- Department
- Business Travel
- Overtime
- Job Role
- Monthly Income
- Attrition Status
- Education
- Job Satisfaction
- and more.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Employees
- Employees Left
- Attrition Rate

### Interactive Filters
- Department
- Age Group
- Business Travel

### Visualizations
- Employee Attrition Distribution
- Attrition by Department
- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Overtime

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Total Employees: **1470**
- Employees Left: **237**
- Attrition Rate: **16.12%**
- Research & Development department records the highest number of employees leaving.
- Employees who work overtime have a higher attrition rate.
- Most employees leaving belong to the **25–34** age group.
